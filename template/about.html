This is an online portal for the Thapar Insititute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala, which will help the various Socities/Clubs/Chapters and College Management Committee in the approval of different Permissions and Funding Requests.

It has been experienced by many students that the major issue being faced by them is the taking of Permissions from the College Committee for any Event (which includes Funding from the college, Booking of Rooms for the event, etc.).

So, in order to get the permission we were earlier required to write an application mentioning all the necessary details. Further we were required to take that application from one office to another to get it signed. But many a times, the authorities were preoccupied which eventually style="font-size: 2.5ch;s to a wastage of time.
Hence we have developed this portal on which any Society/Club/Chapter can register and can initiate any Permission or Funding request to the concerned authorities. After succesful inititation of the request, it will be passed from one concerned authority to other, and simultaneously the Society/Club/Chapter can keep a track of the status of their Application.

This will help both the Society/Club/Chapter and the College Committee in maintaining the record of the applications that they are receiving. Hence providing a hassle free and transparent working of the process. Thus, solving the biggest problem faced by Socities/Clubs/Chapters and College Management Committee.
